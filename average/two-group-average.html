Group Average - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: Group A has 10 students with an average score of 70. Group B has 15 students with an average score of 80. Find the average score when both groups are combined.
Sum of Group A = \(10 \times 70 = 700\)
Sum of Group B = \(15 \times 80 = 1200\)
Total sum = \(700 + 1200 = 1900\)
Total students = \(10 + 15 = 25\)
Combined average = \(1900/25 = 76\)
Answer: 76
Q2: Group X has 12 people with an average income of 50,000. Group Y has 18 people with an average income of 60,000. Find the combined average income.
Sum of X = \(12 \times 50000 = 600000\)
Sum of Y = \(18 \times 60000 = 1080000\)
Total sum = \(1680000\)
Total people = \(12 + 18 = 30\)
Combined average = \(1680000 / 30 = 56000\)
Answer: 56,000
Q3: Two groups have averages 65 and 75 respectively. If each group has equal number of students, find the overall average.
Since equal number of students, overall average = \((65 + 75)/2 = 70\)
Answer: 70
Q4: Group A has 8 students with average 78, and Group B has 12 students with average 82. If one more student with score 90 joins Group B, find the new combined average.
Sum A = \(8 \times 78 = 624\)
Original Sum B = \(12 \times 82 = 984\)
New Sum B = \(984 + 90 = 1074\)
Total sum = \(624 + 1074 = 1698\)
Total students = \(8 + 13 = 21\)
Combined average = \(1698/21 \approx 80.86\)
Answer: 80.86
Q5: A class of 20 students has an average of 75. Another class of 25 students has an average of 80. Find the difference between the combined average and the average of the larger class.
Sum of class1 = \(20 \times 75 = 1500\)
Sum of class2 = \(25 \times 80 = 2000\)
Total sum = 3500, total students = 45
Combined average = \(3500 / 45 \approx 77.78\)
Difference = \(77.78 - 80 = -2.22\)
Answer: -2.22
Q6: Group A has 15 students with average marks 68, Group B has 10 students with average 72, and Group C has 5 students with average 80. Find the overall average.
Sum A = \(15 \times 68 = 1020\)
Sum B = \(10 \times 72 = 720\)
Sum C = \(5 \times 80 = 400\)
Total sum = \(1020 + 720 + 400 = 2140\)
Total students = 15 + 10 + 5 = 30
Combined average = \(2140 / 30 \approx 71.33\)
Answer: 71.33
Q7: Two groups have averages 50 and 70. If the combined average is 60, find the ratio of number of students in the two groups.
Let number of students = x and y
\((50x + 70y)/(x+y) = 60 \Rightarrow 50x + 70y = 60x + 60y \Rightarrow 10y = 10x \Rightarrow x:y = 1:1\)
Answer: 1:1
Q8: Group X has 12 people with average 40. Group Y has 18 people with average 50. A new person joins Group X with score 70. Find the new combined average of X and Y.
Sum X original = \(12 \times 40 = 480\)
New sum X = 480 + 70 = 550
Sum Y = \(18 \times 50 = 900\)
Total sum = 550 + 900 = 1450
Total people = 13 + 18 = 31
Combined average = \(1450 / 31 \approx 46.77\)
Answer: 46.77
Q9: Class A has 20 students with average 65, Class B has 30 students with average 70, Class C has 25 students with average 75. Find the overall average.
Sum A = 20 × 65 = 1300
Sum B = 30 × 70 = 2100
Sum C = 25 × 75 = 1875
Total sum = 1300 + 2100 + 1875 = 5275
Total students = 20 + 30 + 25 = 75
Combined average = 5275 / 75 ≈ 70.33
Answer: 70.33
