Average Speed (Equal Distance) - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: A car travels 100 km at 50 km/h and returns the same distance at 70 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{2 \cdot 50 \cdot 70}{50 + 70} = \frac{7000}{120} \approx 58.33 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 58.33 km/h

Answer: 68.57 km/h
