Basic Average - TCS NQT Numeric Ability

Q1: Find the average of 10, 20, 30, 40, 50.
Step 1: Sum the numbers: \\(10+20+30+40+50 = 150\\)
Step 2: Divide by number of terms: \\(150/5 = 30\\)
Answer: 30
