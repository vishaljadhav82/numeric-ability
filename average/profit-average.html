Profit Average Problems - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: Average profit of 5 shops is ₹2000. One shop earns ₹2500. Find the average profit of remaining 4 shops.
Total profit = 5 × 2000 = ₹10000
Remaining total = 10000 - 2500 = ₹7500
Average = 7500 / 4 = ₹1875
Answer: ₹1875
Q2: Average profit of 10 traders is ₹1500. A new trader joins earning ₹2000. Find the new average.
Total = 10 × 1500 = ₹15000
New total = 15000 + 2000 = ₹17000
New average = 17000 / 11 ≈ ₹1545.45
Answer: ₹1545.45
Q3: Average profit of a company for 6 months is ₹5000. In the 7th month, profit is ₹6500. Find new average.
Total = 6 × 5000 = ₹30000
New total = 30000 + 6500 = ₹36500
New average = 36500 / 7 ≈ ₹5214.29
Answer: ₹5214.29
Q4: Average profit of 8 products is ₹1200. One product has profit ₹1500. Find the average of remaining 7 products.
Total = 8 × 1200 = ₹9600
Remaining total = 9600 - 1500 = ₹8100
Average = 8100 / 7 ≈ ₹1157.14
Answer: ₹1157.14

Q6: Average profit of 7 traders is ₹2200. Two traders earn ₹2500 and ₹2700. Find average profit of remaining 5 traders.
