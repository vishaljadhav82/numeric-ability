Add or Remove Type Average Questions - TCS NQT

Q1: The average of 5 numbers is 20. If a new number 30 is added, what is the new average?
Sum of 5 numbers = \(5 \times 20 = 100\)
Add new number: \(100 + 30 = 130\)
New average = \(130/6 \approx 21.67\)
Answer: 21.67
Q2: Average of 6 numbers is 15. If one number 18 is removed, find the new average.
Sum of 6 numbers = \(6 \times 15 = 90\)
Remove 18: \(90 - 18 = 72\)
New average = \(72 / 5 = 14.4\)
Answer: 14.4
Q3: Average of 8 numbers is 25. Two numbers 30 and 35 are added. Find new average.
Sum of 8 numbers = \(8 \times 25 = 200\)
Add 30 and 35: \(200+30+35 = 265\)
New average = \(265 / 10 = 26.5\)
Answer: 26.5
Q4: Average of 10 numbers is 40. One number 50 is removed. Find the new average.

Answer: 38.89
