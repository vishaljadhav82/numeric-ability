Age Problems - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: The average age of 5 people is 24 years. If one person aged 30 leaves, find the new average.
Total age = 5 × 24 = 120
New total age = 120 - 30 = 90
New average = 90 / 4 = 22.5
Answer: 22.5 years
Q2: Average age of 6 children is 10 years. One more child joins with age 12. Find the new average.
Total age = 6 × 10 = 60
New total age = 60 + 12 = 72
New average = 72 / 7 ≈ 10.29
Answer: 10.29 years
Q3: The average age of a father and his son is 35 years. If the father is 30 years older than the son, find their ages.
Let son's age = x → father = x + 30
Average = (x + x + 30)/2 = 35 → (2x + 30)/2 = 35 → 2x + 30 = 70 → 2x = 40 → x = 20
Father = 20 + 30 = 50
Answer: Son = 20, Father = 50
Q4: Average age of 10 students in a class is 12 years. If teacher's age is included, average becomes 14 years. Find the teacher's age.
Total age of students = 10 × 12 = 120
Total age with teacher = 11 × 14 = 154
Teacher's age = 154 - 120 = 34
Answer: 34 years
Q5: Average age of 3 men is 40 years. When a 30-year-old man is replaced by a 50-year-old man, find the new average.
Original total = 3 × 40 = 120
New total = 120 - 30 + 50 = 140
New average = 140 / 3 ≈ 46.67
Answer: 46.67 years
Q6: Average age of A, B, and C is 24. Average age of A and B is 20. Find C's age.
Total age of A+B+C = 3 × 24 = 72
Total age of A+B = 2 × 20 = 40
C = 72 - 40 = 32
Answer: 32 years
Q7: Average age of a group of 8 people is 28 years. If two people aged 30 and 32 leave, find the new average.
Total age = 8 × 28 = 224
New total = 224 - (30 + 32) = 162
New average = 162 / 6 = 27
Answer: 27 years
Q8: Average age of husband, wife, and child is 28 years. Average age of husband and wife is 32 years. Find the child's age.
Total age of three = 3 × 28 = 84
Total age of husband + wife = 2 × 32 = 64
Child's age = 84 - 64 = 20
Answer: 20 years
Q9: Average age of 5 men is 30 years. If one man aged 40 is replaced by a man aged 50, find the new average.
Original total = 5 × 30 = 150
New total = 150 - 40 + 50 = 160
New average = 160 / 5 = 32
Answer: 32 years
Q10: Average age of 6 boys is 12 years. If their teacher’s age is included, average becomes 16. Find teacher’s age.
Total age of boys = 6 × 12 = 72
Total age with teacher = 7 × 16 = 112
Teacher's age = 112 - 72 = 40
Answer: 40 years
