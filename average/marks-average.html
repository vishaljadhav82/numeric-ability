Marks Average Problems - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: Average marks of 10 students is 72. One student scored 90. Find the average marks of the remaining 9 students.
Total marks = 10 × 72 = 720
Total of remaining 9 = 720 - 90 = 630
Average of remaining 9 = 630 / 9 = 70
Answer: 70 marks
Q2: Average marks of a class of 15 students is 60. A new student joins with 75 marks. Find the new average.
Total marks = 15 × 60 = 900
New total = 900 + 75 = 975
New average = 975 / 16 ≈ 60.94
Answer: 60.94 marks
Q3: Average marks of 5 subjects is 80. If marks in 6th subject is 90, find the new average.
Total marks for 5 subjects = 5 × 80 = 400
Total including 6th = 400 + 90 = 490
New average = 490 / 6 ≈ 81.67
Answer: 81.67 marks
Q4: Average marks of a class of 20 students is 55. Two students scored 65 and 75. Find the average marks of the remaining 18 students.
Total marks = 20 × 55 = 1100
Total of remaining 18 = 1100 - (65 + 75) = 960
Average = 960 / 18 ≈ 53.33
Answer: 53.33 marks
Q5: Average marks of 8 students is 70. One student scored 50. Find the new average of remaining students.
Total marks = 8 × 70 = 560
Remaining total = 560 - 50 = 510
New average = 510 / 7 ≈ 72.86
Answer: 72.86 marks
Q6: Average marks of a class of 10 students is 68. One student scored 80. Find the average of the remaining 9.
Total marks = 10 × 68 = 680
Remaining total = 680 - 80 = 600
Average = 600 / 9 ≈ 66.67
Answer: 66.67 marks
Q7: Average marks of 12 students is 75. One student leaves, average becomes 74. Find the marks of the student who left.
Total marks = 12 × 75 = 900
New total = 11 × 74 = 814
Marks of student who left = 900 - 814 = 86
Answer: 86 marks
Q8: Average marks of 5 students is 60. If a new student scores 80, find the new average.
Total marks = 5 × 60 = 300
New total = 300 + 80 = 380
New average = 380 / 6 ≈ 63.33
Answer: 63.33 marks
Q9: Average marks of 15 students is 72. One student scored 60 and leaves. Find the new average.
Total = 15 × 72 = 1080
Remaining total = 1080 - 60 = 1020
New average = 1020 / 14 ≈ 72.86
Answer: 72.86 marks
Q10: Average marks of a class of 20 students is 65. A student scoring 85 joins. Find the new average.
Total = 20 × 65 = 1300
New total = 1300 + 85 = 1385
New average = 1385 / 21 ≈ 65.95
Answer: 65.95 marks
