Cricket Average Questions - TCS NQT

Q1: A batsman scores 600 runs in 15 innings. Find his average.
Average = Total runs / Innings = 600 / 15 = 40
Answer: 40
Q2: A batsman has an average of 45 after 20 innings. He scores 30 in the next innings. Find his new average.
Total runs = 20 × 45 = 900
Add next score: 900 + 30 = 930
Total innings = 21
New average = 930 / 21 ≈ 44.29
Answer: 44.29
Q3: A bowler has taken 40 wickets at an average of 20. He takes 5 wickets for 50 runs in the next match. Find his updated bowling average.
Total runs conceded = 40 × 20 = 800
Add next match: 800 + 50 = 850
Total wickets = 40 + 5 = 45
New bowling average = 850 / 45 ≈ 18.89
Answer: 18.89
Q4: A batsman’s average is 50 after 10 innings. He scores 0 in the next innings, and his average drops to 45. Find total runs scored in first 10 innings.
Total runs after 11 innings = 11 × 45 = 495
So, runs in first 10 innings = 495 - 0 = 495
Answer: 495 runs
Q5: A bowler’s average is 22 after 50 wickets. He takes 6 wickets for 120 runs in the next match. Find his new average.
Total runs conceded = 50 × 22 = 1100
Add next match: 1100 + 120 = 1220
Total wickets = 50 + 6 = 56
New bowling average = 1220 / 56 ≈ 21.79
Answer: 21.79
Q6: A batsman scores 45, 60, 30, and 75 in four consecutive innings. Find his average.
Total runs = 45 + 60 + 30 + 75 = 210
Average = 210 / 4 = 52.5
Answer: 52.5
Q7: A bowler has conceded 900 runs for 45 wickets. Find his bowling average.
Bowling average = Total runs / Wickets = 900 / 45 = 20
Answer: 20
Q8: A batsman has scored 1200 runs in 25 innings. What is his average?
Average = 1200 / 25 = 48
Answer: 48
Q9: A bowler has an average of 18 after 60 wickets. He takes 6 wickets for 120 runs in the next match. Find his updated bowling average.
Total runs = 60 × 18 = 1080
Add next match: 1080 + 120 = 1200
Total wickets = 60 + 6 = 66
New bowling average = 1200 / 66 ≈ 18.18
Answer: 18.18
Q10: A batsman has an average of 40 after 15 innings. He scores 20, 50, and 30 in the next three innings. Find his new average.
Total runs = 15 × 40 = 600
Add new scores: 600 + 20 + 50 + 30 = 700
Total innings = 18
New average = 700 / 18 ≈ 38.89
Answer: 38.89
