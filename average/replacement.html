Replacement Type Average Questions - TCS NQT

Q1: Average of 5 numbers is 20. One number 25 is replaced by 35. Find the new average.
Original sum = \(5 \times 20 = 100\)
Remove 25 and add 35: \(100 - 25 + 35 = 110\)
New average = \(110 / 5 = 22\)
Answer: 22
Q2: Average of 6 numbers is 15. A number 10 is replaced by 20. Find the new average.
Original sum = \(6 \times 15 = 90\)
New sum = \(90 - 10 + 20 = 100\)
New average = \(100 / 6 \approx 16.67\)
Answer: 16.67
Q3: Average of 8 numbers is 50. Two numbers 45 and 55 are replaced by 60 and 40. Find new average.
Original sum = \(8 \times 50 = 400\)
New sum = \(400 - (45+55) + (60+40) = 400\)
New average = \(400 / 8 = 50\)
Answer: 50
