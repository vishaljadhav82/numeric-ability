Mixed Average Questions - TCS NQT

Q1: A batsman scores 45, 60, 30, and 75 runs in four innings. Find his average.
Total runs = 45 + 60 + 30 + 75 = 210
Average = 210 / 4 = 52.5
Answer: 52.5
Q2: Average salary of 5 employees is ₹20,000. Find total salary.
Total salary = 5 × 20,000 = ₹1,00,000
Answer: ₹1,00,000
Q3: A car travels 60 km at 30 km/h and 90 km at 60 km/h. Find average speed.
Total Distance = 60 + 90 = 150 km
Time1 = 60/30 = 2 h, Time2 = 90/60 = 1.5 h
Total Time = 2 + 1.5 = 3.5 h
Average Speed = 150 / 3.5 ≈ 42.86 km/h
Answer: 42.86 km/h
Q4: A bowler has taken 40 wickets at an average of 20. He takes 5 wickets for 50 runs in the next match. Find his updated bowling average.
Total runs = 40 × 20 = 800
Add next match: 800 + 50 = 850
Total wickets = 40 + 5 = 45
New bowling average = 850 / 45 ≈ 18.89
Answer: 18.89
Q5: Average salary of 8 employees is ₹25,000. One employee joins with ₹30,000. Find new average.
Total salary = 8 × 25,000 = 2,00,000
Add new salary: 2,00,000 + 30,000 = 2,30,000
Total employees = 9
New average = 2,30,000 / 9 ≈ ₹25,555.56
Answer: ₹25,555.56
Q6: A cyclist rides 30 km at 10 km/h and 45 km at 15 km/h. Find average speed.
Total Distance = 30 + 45 = 75 km
Time1 = 30/10 = 3 h, Time2 = 45/15 = 3 h
Total Time = 3 + 3 = 6 h
Average Speed = 75 / 6 = 12.5 km/h
Answer: 12.5 km/h
Q7: A batsman has an average of 45 after 20 innings. He scores 30 in the next innings. Find his new average.
Total runs = 20 × 45 = 900
Add next score: 900 + 30 = 930
Total innings = 21
New average = 930 / 21 ≈ 44.29
Answer: 44.29
Q8: Average salary of 10 employees is ₹28,000. Two leave with salaries ₹30,000 and ₹26,000. Find new average.
Total salary = 10 × 28,000 = 2,80,000
After leaving: 2,80,000 - 30,000 - 26,000 = 2,24,000
Remaining employees = 8
New average = 2,24,000 / 8 = ₹28,000
Answer: ₹28,000
Q9: A vehicle covers 150 km at 50 km/h and 50 km at 25 km/h. Find average speed.
Total Distance = 150 + 50 = 200 km
Time1 = 150/50 = 3 h, Time2 = 50/25 = 2 h
Total Time = 3 + 2 = 5 h
Average Speed = 200 / 5 = 40 km/h
Answer: 40 km/h
Q10: A bowler has an average of 18 after 60 wickets. He takes 6 wickets for 120 runs in the next match. Find updated average.
Total runs = 60 × 18 = 1080
Add next match: 1080 + 120 = 1200
Total wickets = 60 + 6 = 66
New bowling average = 1200 / 66 ≈ 18.18
Answer: 18.18
