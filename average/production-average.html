Production Average Problems - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: Average production of 5 machines is 120 units/day. One machine produces 150 units/day. Find the average production of remaining 4 machines.
Total production = 5 × 120 = 600 units
Remaining total = 600 - 150 = 450 units
Average = 450 / 4 = 112.5 units/day
Answer: 112.5 units/day
Q2: Average output of 10 workers is 80 units/day. A new worker joins producing 100 units/day. Find new average.
Total = 10 × 80 = 800 units
New total = 800 + 100 = 900 units
New average = 900 / 11 ≈ 81.82 units/day
Answer: 81.82 units/day
Q3: Average daily production of a factory for 7 days is 200 units. On the 8th day, production is 250 units. Find the new average.
Total = 7 × 200 = 1400 units
New total = 1400 + 250 = 1650 units
New average = 1650 / 8 ≈ 206.25 units/day
Answer: 206.25 units/day
Q4: Average production of 6 machines is 180 units/day. One machine fails and produces 150 units. Find new average production.
Total = 6 × 180 = 1080 units
Total excluding failing machine = 1080 - 180 + 150 = 1050 units
New average = 1050 / 6 = 175 units/day
Answer: 175 units/day
Q5: Average production of 12 workers is 150 units/day. One worker leaves producing 180 units/day. Find new average.

Q6: Average production of 8 machines is 200 units/day. Two new machines produce 220 and 240 units/day. Find new average.
