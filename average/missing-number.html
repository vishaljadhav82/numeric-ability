Advanced Missing Number in Average - TCS NQT

Q1: The average of 5 numbers is 23. Four of the numbers are 20, 26, 25, 22. Find the missing number.
Let missing number = \(x\)
Sum of 4 numbers = \(20+26+25+22=93\)
Total sum needed = \(5 \times 23 = 115\)
Missing number: \(x = 115-93 = 22\)
Answer: 22
Q2: Average of 6 numbers is 45. Five numbers are 40, 50, 42, 48, 45. Find the missing number.
Sum of 5 numbers = \(40+50+42+48+45=225\)
Total sum = \(6 \times 45 = 270\)
Missing number = \(270-225 = 45\)
Answer: 45
