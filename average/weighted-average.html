Weighted Average - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: A student scored 80 in Maths, 90 in Physics, and 70 in Chemistry. If the weights of the subjects are 3, 4, and 2 respectively, find the weighted average.
Weighted average = \(\frac{(80×3)+(90×4)+(70×2)}{3+4+2} = \frac{240+360+140}{9} = 740/9 \approx 82.22\)
Answer: 82.22
Q2: In a class, 20 students scored 60 marks, 15 students scored 75 marks, and 10 students scored 90 marks. Find the weighted average of the class.
Weighted average = \(\frac{(20×60)+(15×75)+(10×90)}{20+15+10} = \frac{1200+1125+900}{45} = 3225/45 = 71.67\)
Answer: 71.67
Q3: A shop sold 10 kg of rice at $50/kg, 15 kg at $60/kg, and 20 kg at $55/kg. Find the weighted average price per kg.
Weighted average = \(\frac{(10×50)+(15×60)+(20×55)}{10+15+20} = \frac{500+900+1100}{45} = 2500/45 \approx 55.56\)
Answer: 55.56
Q4: In a test, two papers have weights 40% and 60%. A student scored 75 in the first paper and 85 in the second. Find the weighted average.
Weighted average = \((75×0.4) + (85×0.6) = 30 + 51 = 81\)
Answer: 81
Q5: Three batches of items have prices $10, $12, and $15. Quantities are 50, 30, and 20 respectively. Find the weighted average price.
Weighted average = \(\frac{(50×10)+(30×12)+(20×15)}{50+30+20} = \frac{500+360+300}{100} = 1160/100 = 11.6\)
Answer: 11.6
Q6: A student’s marks in 4 subjects are 70, 80, 90, 85. The weights are 1, 2, 3, 4 respectively. Find the weighted average.
Weighted average = \(\frac{(70×1)+(80×2)+(90×3)+(85×4)}{1+2+3+4} = \frac{70+160+270+340}{10} = 840/10 = 84\)
Answer: 84
Q7: Three friends scored 60, 75, 90. Their contributions to a group project are 20%, 30%, 50%. Find the weighted average score.
Weighted average = \((60×0.2) + (75×0.3) + (90×0.5) = 12 + 22.5 + 45 = 79.5\)
Answer: 79.5
Q8: In a factory, 100 units of A are produced at $20/unit, 150 units of B at $30/unit, and 50 units of C at $25/unit. Find the weighted average cost per unit.
Weighted average = \(\frac{(100×20)+(150×30)+(50×25)}{100+150+50} = \frac{2000+4500+1250}{300} = 7750/300 \approx 25.83\)
Answer: 25.83
Q9: A student’s marks in 3 tests are 50, 60, 70. Tests are weighted as 2, 3, 5. Find the weighted average.
Weighted average = \(\frac{(50×2)+(60×3)+(70×5)}{2+3+5} = \frac{100+180+350}{10} = 630/10 = 63\)
Answer: 63
Q10: A company has 3 products sold in quantities 200, 300, 500 units at prices 15, 20, 25 respectively. Find the weighted average price.
Weighted average = \(\frac{(200×15)+(300×20)+(500×25)}{200+300+500} = \frac{3000+6000+12500}{1000} = 21500/1000 = 21.5\)
Answer: 21.5
