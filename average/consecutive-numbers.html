Consecutive Numbers Average - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: Find the average of first 10 natural numbers.
First 10 numbers: 1,2,…,10
Average = (1+10)/2 = 11/2 = 5.5
Answer: 5.5
Q2: The average of 5 consecutive numbers is 20. Find the numbers.
Let numbers = \(x-2, x-1, x, x+1, x+2\)
Average = x = 20 → middle number = 20
Numbers = 18,19,20,21,22
Answer: 18,19,20,21,22
Q3: Find the average of first 15 odd numbers.
First 15 odd numbers: 1,3,5,…,29
Average = (1+29)/2 = 30/2 = 15
Answer: 15
Q4: Five consecutive even numbers have an average of 26. Find the numbers.
Let numbers = x-4, x-2, x, x+2, x+4
Average = x = 26 → middle number = 26
Numbers = 22,24,26,28,30
Answer: 22,24,26,28,30
Q5: The sum of 7 consecutive numbers is 105. Find the numbers.
Average = 105/7 = 15 → middle number = 15
Numbers = 12,13,14,15,16,17,18
Answer: 12,13,14,15,16,17,18
Q6: Average of 20 consecutive numbers is 50. Find the first and last numbers.
Let middle two numbers = 50,51 (even count → two middle numbers)
Numbers = 41,42,…,60
Answer: First = 41, Last = 60
Q7: The sum of 9 consecutive odd numbers is 81. Find the numbers.
Average = 81/9 = 9 → middle number = 9
Numbers = 1,3,5,7,9,11,13,15,17
Answer: 1,3,5,7,9,11,13,15,17
Q8: Average of 6 consecutive numbers is 34. Find the numbers.
Let middle numbers = 33,34 (even count)
Numbers = 31,32,33,34,35,36
Answer: 31,32,33,34,35,36
Q9: The average of 11 consecutive numbers is 27. Find the first and last numbers.
Middle number = 27 → first = 27-5 = 22, last = 27+5 = 32
Answer: First = 22, Last = 32
Q10: Five consecutive integers have a sum of 100. Find the numbers.
Average = 100/5 = 20 → middle number = 20
Numbers = 18,19,20,21,22
Answer: 18,19,20,21,22
