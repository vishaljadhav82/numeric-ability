Average Speed (Unequal Time) - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: A car travels 60 km at 30 km/h and 90 km at 60 km/h. Find average speed.
Total Distance = \(60 + 90 = 150\) km
Time1 = \(60/30 = 2\) h, Time2 = \(90/60 = 1.5\) h
Total Time = \(2 + 1.5 = 3.5\) h
Average Speed = \(150 / 3.5 \approx 42.86\) km/h
Answer: 42.86 km/h
Q2: A train covers 100 km at 50 km/h and 150 km at 75 km/h. Find average speed.
Total Distance = \(100 + 150 = 250\) km
Time1 = \(100/50 = 2\) h, Time2 = \(150/75 = 2\) h
Total Time = \(2 + 2 = 4\) h
Average Speed = \(250/4 = 62.5\) km/h
Answer: 62.5 km/h
