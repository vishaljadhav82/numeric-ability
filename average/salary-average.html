Salary Average Questions - TCS NQT

Q1: Average salary of 5 employees is ₹20,000. Find total salary.
Total salary = Average × Number of employees = 20,000 × 5 = ₹1,00,000
Answer: ₹1,00,000
Q2: Average salary of 8 employees is ₹25,000. One employee with a salary of ₹30,000 joins. Find new average.
Total salary = 8 × 25,000 = 2,00,000
Add new salary: 2,00,000 + 30,000 = 2,30,000
Total employees = 9
New average = 2,30,000 / 9 ≈ ₹25,555.56
Answer: ₹25,555.56
Q3: Average salary of 10 employees is ₹18,000. One leaves with salary ₹20,000. Find new average.
Total salary = 10 × 18,000 = 1,80,000
After leaving: 1,80,000 - 20,000 = 1,60,000
Remaining employees = 9
New average = 1,60,000 / 9 ≈ ₹17,777.78
Answer: ₹17,777.78
Q4: Average salary of 6 employees is ₹15,000. Two new employees with ₹18,000 and ₹20,000 join. Find new average.
Total salary = 6 × 15,000 = 90,000
Add new salaries: 90,000 + 18,000 + 20,000 = 1,28,000
Total employees = 8
New average = 1,28,000 / 8 = ₹16,000
Answer: ₹16,000
Q5: Average salary of 12 employees is ₹22,000. One employee with salary ₹28,000 leaves. Find new average.
Total salary = 12 × 22,000 = 2,64,000
After leaving: 2,64,000 - 28,000 = 2,36,000
Remaining employees = 11
New average = 2,36,000 / 11 ≈ ₹21,454.55
Answer: ₹21,454.55
Q6: Average salary of 7 employees is ₹30,000. One employee is promoted and his salary increases by ₹10,000. Find new average.
Total salary = 7 × 30,000 = 2,10,000
Add increment: 2,10,000 + 10,000 = 2,20,000
Average = 2,20,000 / 7 ≈ ₹31,428.57
Answer: ₹31,428.57
Q7: Average salary of 5 employees is ₹25,000. One employee’s salary decreases by ₹5,000. Find new average.
Total salary = 5 × 25,000 = 1,25,000
Subtract decrease: 1,25,000 - 5,000 = 1,20,000
Average = 1,20,000 / 5 = ₹24,000
Answer: ₹24,000
Q8: Average salary of 9 employees is ₹18,000. One employee leaves with ₹15,000 and another joins with ₹24,000. Find new average.
Total salary = 9 × 18,000 = 1,62,000
After leaving & joining: 1,62,000 - 15,000 + 24,000 = 1,71,000
Total employees = 9
New average = 1,71,000 / 9 = ₹19,000
Answer: ₹19,000
Q9: Average salary of 4 employees is ₹40,000. Three new employees with ₹35,000, ₹45,000, and ₹50,000 join. Find new average.
Total salary = 4 × 40,000 = 1,60,000
Add new salaries: 1,60,000 + 35,000 + 45,000 + 50,000 = 2,90,000
Total employees = 7
New average = 2,90,000 / 7 ≈ ₹41,428.57
Answer: ₹41,428.57
Q10: Average salary of 10 employees is ₹28,000. Two leave with salaries ₹30,000 and ₹26,000. Find new average.
Total salary = 10 × 28,000 = 2,80,000
After leaving: 2,80,000 - 30,000 - 26,000 = 2,24,000
Remaining employees = 8
New average = 2,24,000 / 8 = ₹28,000
