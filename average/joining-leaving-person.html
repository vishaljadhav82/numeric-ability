Joining/Leaving Person Problems - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: Average age of 10 people is 24. One person aged 30 leaves. Find the new average.
Total age = 10 × 24 = 240
New total = 240 - 30 = 210
New average = 210 / 9 ≈ 23.33
Answer: 23.33 years
Q2: Average of 12 students is 18 years. A new student joins with age 20. Find the new average.
Total age = 12 × 18 = 216
New total = 216 + 20 = 236
New average = 236 / 13 ≈ 18.15
Answer: 18.15 years
Q3: Average age of 8 people is 30. One person leaves and new average becomes 29. Find the age of the person who left.
Original total = 8 × 30 = 240
New total = 7 × 29 = 203
Age of person who left = 240 - 203 = 37
Answer: 37 years
Q4: Average of 15 people is 20. Two new people aged 25 and 35 join. Find the new average.
Original total = 15 × 20 = 300
New total = 300 + 25 + 35 = 360
New average = 360 / 17 ≈ 21.18
Answer: 21.18 years
Q5: Average of 6 men is 40. One man leaves and average becomes 38. Find the age of the man who left.
Total = 6 × 40 = 240
New total = 5 × 38 = 190
Age of man who left = 240 - 190 = 50
Answer: 50 years
Q6: Average of 10 students is 16. A new student joins and new average becomes 17. Find the new student’s age.
Total = 10 × 16 = 160
New total = 11 × 17 = 187
Age of new student = 187 - 160 = 27
Answer: 27 years
Q7: Average of 5 men is 50. One man leaves and average becomes 48. Find the age of the man who left.
Total = 5 × 50 = 250
New total = 4 × 48 = 192
Age of man who left = 250 - 192 = 58
Answer: 58 years
Q8: Average of 20 students is 15. Two students aged 18 and 22 leave. Find new average.
Total = 20 × 15 = 300
New total = 300 - (18+22) = 260
New average = 260 / 18 ≈ 14.44
Answer: 14.44 years
Q9: Average of 12 people is 28. One person aged 34 joins. Find new average.
Total = 12 × 28 = 336
New total = 336 + 34 = 370
New average = 370 / 13 ≈ 28.46
Answer: 28.46 years
Q10: Average of 8 students is 25. One leaves and average becomes 24. Find the age of the student who left.

New total = 7 × 24 = 168
