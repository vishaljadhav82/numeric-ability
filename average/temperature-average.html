Temperature Average Problems - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: Average temperature of 5 days is 30°C. On one day it was 35°C. Find the average of the remaining 4 days.
Total temperature = 5 × 30 = 150°C
Remaining total = 150 - 35 = 115°C
Average = 115 / 4 = 28.75°C
Answer: 28.75°C
Q2: Average temperature of a week (7 days) is 25°C. A new 8th day has 30°C. Find the new average.
Total = 7 × 25 = 175°C
New total = 175 + 30 = 205°C
New average = 205 / 8 ≈ 25.625°C
Answer: 25.625°C
Q3: Average temperature of Monday to Friday is 32°C. Saturday temperature is 36°C. Find the new average.
Total Mon-Fri = 5 × 32 = 160°C
New total = 160 + 36 = 196°C
Average = 196 / 6 ≈ 32.67°C
Answer: 32.67°C
Q4: Average temperature of 6 days is 28°C. On one day it was 34°C. Find the average of remaining 5 days.
Total = 6 × 28 = 168°C
Remaining total = 168 - 34 = 134°C
Average = 134 / 5 = 26.8°C
Answer: 26.8°C
Q5: Average temperature of a month of 30 days is 24°C. A new day’s reading of 30°C is recorded. Find the new average.
Total = 30 × 24 = 720°C
New total = 720 + 30 = 750°C
New average = 750 / 31 ≈ 24.19°C
Answer: 24.19°C
Q6: Average temperature of 10 cities is 20°C. One city reports 28°C. Find new average.
Total = 10 × 20 = 200°C
New total = 200 + 28 = 228°C
New average = 228 / 11 ≈ 20.73°C
Answer: 20.73°C
Q7: Average temperature of 5 days is 27°C. One day temperature increases to 33°C. Find new average.
Total = 5 × 27 = 135°C
Replace old value with 33°C → Total = 135 - x + 33 (assume x is old temp, e.g., 27)
New average = (135 - 27 + 33)/5 = 141 / 5 = 28.2°C
Answer: 28.2°C
Q8: Average temperature of 7 days is 22°C. Two days report 25°C and 28°C. Find average of remaining 5 days.
Total = 7 × 22 = 154°C
Remaining total = 154 - (25+28) = 101°C
Average = 101 / 5 = 20.2°C
Answer: 20.2°C
Q9: Average temperature of 12 days is 26°C. A new day of 32°C is recorded. Find new average.
Total = 12 × 26 = 312°C
New total = 312 + 32 = 344°C
New average = 344 / 13 ≈ 26.46°C
Answer: 26.46°C
Q10: Average temperature of 8 days is 30°C. One day leaves out a reading of 36°C. Find new average of remaining 7 days.
Total = 8 × 30 = 240°C
Remaining total = 240 - 36 = 204°C
Average = 204 / 7 ≈ 29.14°C
Answer: 29.14°C
