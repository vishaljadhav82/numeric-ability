Alligation & Average Questions - TCS NQT

Q1: A trader mixes rice at ₹40/kg and ₹60/kg. Find the ratio in which they should be mixed to get ₹50/kg.
Alligation method: \(\text{High price} - \text{Mean} = 60 - 50 = 10\)
\(\text{Mean} - \text{Low price} = 50 - 40 = 10\)
Ratio = 10:10 = 1:1
Answer: 1:1
Q2: Milk at 8% fat is mixed with milk at 12% fat to get 10% fat. Find ratio.
Alligation: 12 - 10 = 2
10 - 8 = 2
Ratio = 2:2 = 1:1
Answer: 1:1
Q3: Sugar at ₹30/kg is mixed with sugar at ₹50/kg to get ₹40/kg. Ratio?
50 - 40 = 10
40 - 30 = 10
Ratio = 10:10 = 1:1
Answer: 1:1
Q4: Alcohol 15% is mixed with alcohol 25% to get 20%. Find ratio.
25 - 20 = 5
20 - 15 = 5
Ratio = 5:5 = 1:1
Answer: 1:1
Q5: Copper at ₹120/kg is mixed with copper at ₹80/kg to get ₹100/kg. Ratio?
120 - 100 = 20
100 - 80 = 20
Ratio = 20:20 = 1:1
Answer: 1:1
Q6: Tea at ₹200/kg is mixed with tea at ₹150/kg to get ₹170/kg. Ratio?
200 - 170 = 30
170 - 150 = 20
Ratio = 30:20 = 3:2
Answer: 3:2
Q7: Gold at ₹50,000/gm is mixed with ₹48,000/gm to get ₹49,000/gm. Find ratio.
50,000 - 49,000 = 1,000
49,000 - 48,000 = 1,000
Ratio = 1:1
Answer: 1:1
Q8: Milk at 6% fat is mixed with milk at 9% fat to get 7% fat. Ratio?
9 - 7 = 2
7 - 6 = 1
Ratio = 2:1
Answer: 2:1
Q9: Two types of wheat at ₹25/kg and ₹30/kg are mixed to get ₹27/kg. Find ratio.
30 - 27 = 3
27 - 25 = 2
Ratio = 3:2
Answer: 3:2
Q10: Sugar solution 20% is mixed with 50% to get 35%. Ratio?
50 - 35 = 15
35 - 20 = 15
Ratio = 15:15 = 1:1
Answer: 1:1
