Average Speed (Equal Time) - TCS NQT Practice

Q1: A car travels for 2 hours at 60 km/h and 2 hours at 90 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{60 + 90}{2} = 75 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 75 km/h
Q2: A train runs 3 hours at 40 km/h and 3 hours at 60 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{40 + 60}{2} = 50 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 50 km/h
Q3: A cyclist rides 1 hour at 15 km/h, 1 hour at 20 km/h, and 1 hour at 25 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{15 + 20 + 25}{3} = 20 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 20 km/h
Q4: A car travels 2 hours at 50 km/h and 2 hours at 70 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{50 + 70}{2} = 60 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 60 km/h
Q5: A train runs equal time at 80 km/h and 120 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{80 + 120}{2} = 100 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 100 km/h
Q6: A boat goes 2 hours downstream at 30 km/h and 2 hours upstream at 20 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{30 + 20}{2} = 25 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 25 km/h
Q7: A car drives 1 hour at 45 km/h, 1 hour at 60 km/h, and 1 hour at 75 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{45 + 60 + 75}{3} = 60 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 60 km/h
Q8: A motorbike runs 2 hours at 36 km/h and 2 hours at 54 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{36 + 54}{2} = 45 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 45 km/h
Q9: A vehicle moves 3 hours at 50 km/h and 3 hours at 70 km/h. Find average speed.
Average speed = \( \frac{50 + 70}{2} = 60 \text{ km/h} \)
Answer: 60 km/h
